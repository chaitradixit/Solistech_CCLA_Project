CCLA Parking Lot Bundles
Total Clients <$#local.parkingLotClientCount$> Total number of unique clients who have mandates in the Parking Lot queue. If a client has more than 1 envelope/bundle in the queue they will be counted more than once.
Total Accounts <$#local.parkingLotAccountCount$> Total number of unique accounts in the Parking Lot queue. If the same account appears in more than 1 envelope/bundle in the queue, it will be counted more than once.
Total Docs <$#local.parkingLotDocumentCount$> Total number of mandate instructions in the Parking Lot queue.
Total Bundles <$#local.parkingLotBundleCount$> Total number of envelopes/bundles containing mandate instructions in the Parking Lot queue.
Total Bundles (complex) <$#local.parkingLotBundleCount - #local.basicParkingLotBundleCount$> Total number of envelopes/bundles containing more than 1 mandate instruction in the Parking Lot queue.
Total Bundles (basic) <$#local.basicParkingLotBundleCount$> Total number of envelopes/bundles containing exactly 1 mandate instruction in the Parking Lot queue.
